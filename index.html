Simon Weber

I am a PhD candidate at the Computer Vision Group supervised by Prof. Dr. Daniel Cremers at the Technical University of Munich. Before that, I studied at École normale supérieure (ENS Ulm) in Paris.

My research interests include 3D reconstruction for structure-from-motion and SLAM system, beyond-euclidean geometry, and semantic segmentation.
